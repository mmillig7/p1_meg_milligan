Needlepoint has been a hobby of mine for about 2 years, but in the last year I’ve taken it up more seriously.

I got to work at a local needlepoint shop in Nashville over Christmas break, where I got to learn from the pros.

Needlepoint gives me an outlet that doesn’t put any pressure on me. I get to make whatever I want and express my style and passions through it. I love having something physical to have a hold as proof of my effort.
